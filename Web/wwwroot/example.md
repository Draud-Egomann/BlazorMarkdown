## Text Formatting
You can format text in various ways:

- **Bold**
- *Italic*
- ~~Strikethrough~~
- **_Bold and Italic_**

## Lists
There are numbered and bulleted lists:

### Bulleted Lists
- Item 1
- Item 2
  - Subitem 2.1
  - Subitem 2.2

### Numbered Lists
1. First item
2. Second item
   1. Subitem 2.1
   2. Subitem 2.2
